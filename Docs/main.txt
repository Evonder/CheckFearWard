Track the current Fear Ward buffs cast by yourself or any member of your party or raid.

=== Check Fear Ward Description
Check Fear Ward has the ability to track who has cast their Fear Ward buff and how long the buff has left on the person with said buff. It also has the ability to alert you when the buff has been consumed or has expired, and on top of a personal alert it also is able to alert your group by adding a message to say, party or raid.
